Banana Bread Recipe

Ingredients for the banana bread:

  • All purpose flour
  • Banana
  • Egg
  • Butter (Melted)
  • Brown sugar
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Baking powder
  • Baking soda
  • Cinnamon powder
  • Vanilla essence
  • Salt

Step-by-step instructions:

  1. Grease a bread pan with butter or oil and place parchment paper on the bottom.
  2. In a bowl, mash the bananas.
  3. Add melted butter, brown sugar, sugar and eggs into the bowl and stir the mixture until the sugar dissolves.
  4. Now add the milk and vanilla essence and stir the mixture again.
  5. Sift the leftover dry ingredients into the mixture and stir for the final time until everything is mixed well.
  6. Pout the mixture into the greased bread pan and tap the pan to release any bubbles.
  7. Bake in preheated oven (180 ° C) for 45 to 50 mins.
  8. Take your bread out of the oven and let it rest for 10 mins before taking it out of the pan.
