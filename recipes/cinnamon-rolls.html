Cinnamon Rolls Recipe

Ingredients for cinnamon rolls

The dough:

  • Bread flour 300g
  • Milk 160ml
  • Egg 1 medium
  • Sugar 30g
  • Instant dry yeast 4g
  • Salt ½tsp
  • Unsalted butter (room temp) 40g

The filling:

  • Unsalted butter (softened) 50g
  • Brown sugar 70g
  • Cinnamon powder 4g

The frosting:

  • Unsalted butter (room temp) 227g
  • Fine powdered sugar 300-350g
  • Milk (room temp) 75-90ml
  • Salt ⅛tsp
  • Vanilla extract 1 tsp

Step-by-step instructions:

  1. In a bowl, mix the milk, sugar, egg and yeast.
  2. Add the bread flour and salt into the mixture and start mixing to form a dough.
  3. Cover the bowl and let it rest for 30 mins.
  4. Add the butter to the dough and knead it for 5 to 10 mins until the dough becomes smooth.
  5. Grease the dough and bowl then cover and rest until the dough doubles in size.
  6. While the dough rest, in another bowl, add the softened butter, cinnamon powder and brown sugar. Stir everything together until you get a spreadable filling.
  7. After the dough has doubled, flour your surface to roll the dough on.
  8. Spread your filling over the rolled dough surface.
  9. Slice the dough into strips and roll each strip to make the spiral cinnamon rolls.
  10. Grease your baking tray and place the cinnamon rolls in it. (Make sure to leave gaps between the rolls as they will increase in size after proofing)
  11. Cover the tray and let it rest for 50 mins or until the rolls double in size.
  12. Place the baking tray into a preheated 180°C oven for 20 mins.
  13. Let the cinnamon rolls rest and cool down. During that time, make the frosting.
  14. To a mixing bowl, add the butter and using a handheld or stand mixer, bear on high speed for 2-3mins, until creamed and fluffy.
  15. Sift the powdered sugar into the mixing bowl, alongside the vanilla extract and salt.
  16. Beat on slow speed until everything is incorporated, then beat on high speed for 2-3 mins. Start adding the milk gradually, 1 tablespoon at a time, until you reach your desired consistency.
  17. Spread the frosting over the cooled cinnamon rolls.
