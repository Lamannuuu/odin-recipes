Spaghetti Aglio e Olio Recipe

Ingredients for the spaghetti agli-e-olio:

  • Dried spaghetti pasta 160g
  • Extra virgin olive oil 3 tbsp
  • Garlic 2 cloves
  • Red pepper flakes ¼tsp
  • Handful fresh parsley leaves
  • Salt to cook pasta

Step-by-step instructions:

  1. Boil the pasta in a large pot of heavily salted water for 2 minutes under the package instructions and ensure to reserve some pasta water
  2. At the same time, thinly slice the garlic cloves and chop up the parsley
  3. In a cold pan, add the olive oil, sliced garlic and red pepper flakes or fresh chilli. Place it on the stove top over low heat
  4. When the garlic starts to slowly sizzle, add the parsley. Saute together nice and slow, making sure not to burn the garlic
  5. Pour a ladle of pasta cooking water to the pan and stir together
  6. When the pasta is almost cooked but still very al dente, drain and add it straight to the pan with another splash of pasta water - increase to medium heat
  7. Toss the pasta for 2-3 minutes continuously stirring which will emulsify the sauce and become silky and creamy - adding pasta water slowly if it needs it
